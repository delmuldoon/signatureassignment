What's with all the L's?

Certain services require different amounts of labor based on difficulty; the prices reflect that. For a quick rundown:

  • Computer Cleaning: L1 refers to light cleaning, for devices with minor dust buildup or residue. L2 refers to a deep cleaning, involving more specialized equipment.
  • Hardware Installation: L1 refers to RAM cards, fans, and hard drives. L2 refers to I/0 cards, optical drives, and screen displays. L3 refers to power supplies, processors, and motherboards.
  • Malware Cleanup: Sometimes what looks like very bad malware turns out to be very simple to fix, and sometimes malware that looks easy to fix is actually very difficult. As such, I won't know whether it's an L1 or an L2 until I actually start working on the device. If it's beyond my capability and I have to refer you elsewhere, you get no charge.
