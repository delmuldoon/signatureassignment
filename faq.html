Frequently Asked Questions

"So, what's your whole deal?"

What are your hours?

I work a day job, so the hours of 10 AM - 7:30 PM Monday through Friday are mostly off-limits. Depending on the circumstances, I might agree to arrange for an appointment during that timeframe, but it would be at a marked-up price, considering I would have to take PTO. Outside of those hours, I can make myself available whenever is convenient for you, barring previously-made appointments.

Why do you do this if you have a day job?

In short, because I like it. I get to be more flexible in offering services, rather than knowing how to help someone but not being allowed to, due to rules set by my managers at my day job. For example; we don't do data recovery at my job, because it's a hassle and my managers don't want to offer it as a service. I also get to meet people where they're at; I get a sense of accomplishment from providing a service to members of my community who would otherwise have difficulty receiving service.

Do you have a physical shop I can visit?

Sadly, no. Finances-wise, it doesn't make sense for me to pay for a workshop location for something that's a side-gig. I have an at-home workshop setup, but I can't open that up to the public for obvious reasons. Also, I enjoy getting to see different parts of the city; being a mobile repair service brings me to parts of town I would normally never think to visit. Eventually, I want to own and run my own shop full-time, but at the moment I'm still building my career, so that's a long-term goal.

How many technicians do you employ?

It's just me! If I start to get a lot of customers, I might think of hiring an additional technician or two, but for the moment I'm the only one.
